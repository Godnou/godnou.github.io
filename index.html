Ferromagnetic elastomer lattices

2019-04-08
3DPrinting

A ferromagnetic elastomer octet-truss lattice is deformed subject to a magnetic field. This is one of my favorite research projects during the time I was studying at the USC.

A theoretical analysis on the phenomena is given as following: 'Magnetic Field controlled Buckling and Postbuckling of an Inclined Beam'

I left the USC in July 2017. Unfortunately, I did not have time to finish the rest of this research project and many others.

Anyway, an excellent work based on the ferromagnetic elasomers is finally done by the successors. Reference: Magnetoactive acoustic metamaterials (Big congrats!)
